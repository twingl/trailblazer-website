Trailblazer

Trailblazer maps out your journey across the web so you never get lost.
Join the Webstock Preview.

Familiar sight?

Researching online is a messy process. You start with a search, click a few links... before you know it, it's three hours later, the sun is setting, you've got a hundred tabs open and feel completely lost.

Trailblazer is an addon for Google Chrome which turns your hundreds of tabs into a glanceable, shareable map. Sign up for the beta.

  1. 1. Just start recording.

    Trailblazer will follow you down the rabbit hole. As you click links and run searches, it builds up a map of your journey.

  2. 2. Lost? Pull out your map.

    Trailblazer shows you what’s open, where you’ve been, and what was good.

  3. 3. Come back to your trail.

    Feel free to close all of your tabs. The map makes it super easy to pick up where you left off. 3 minutes or 3 months later.

  4. 4. Share what you’ve learned.

    Got a friend or co-worker researching something similar? Give them a head start by sharing them your trail.

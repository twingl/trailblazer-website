Trailblazer

Trailblazer maps out your journey across the web so you never get lost.
Sign up for the beta.

Familiar sight?

Researching online is a messy process. You start with a search, click a few links... before you know it, it's three hours later, the sun is setting, you've got a hundred tabs open and feel completely lost.

Trailblazer turns your hundreds of tabs into a glanceable, shareable map. Sign up for the beta.

  1. 1. Just start recording.

    Trailblazer will make order out of your chaos. It’ll even grab the tabs you open to Google something new.

  2. 2. Find your way.

    You get a glanceable map, showing you what’s open, what you’ve read, and what was important.

  3. 3. Pick up the trail.

    Feel free to declare tab bankruptcy. With a map, you can pick up where you left off—whether it’s 3 minutes or 3 months later.

  4. 4. Share what you’ve learned.

    Got a friend or co-worker researching something similar? Give them a head start by sharing them your trail.
